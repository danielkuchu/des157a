How to play:

  • You are randomly assigned a pokemon after you click "begin."
  • From there, the first turn will be randomly assigned to Pikachu or Charmander, player 1 and player 2.
  • There are 7 outcomes from clicking the roll button:
    1. Weak attack (-10 HP)
    2. Strong attack (-25 HP)
    3. Player got scared (turn skipped)
    4. Player used steroids (+ 10 HP)
    5. Attack missed (turn skipped)
    6. Player feeling shy (turn skipped)
    7. Player got confused and attacked themselves (-15 HP)
  • Each player starts with 100 HP. First player to reach 0 HP loses. Happy battling!
